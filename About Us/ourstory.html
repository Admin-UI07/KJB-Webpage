Our Story

At Kuya Jepoy - Bigasan, our journey is more than just selling rice—it’s about cultivating a tradition of nourishment, sustainability, and community. Our story began with a simple belief: that quality rice is the foundation of a good meal, and a good meal brings people together.


Our Humble Beginnings


What started as a small family venture quickly grew into a mission-driven business dedicated to providing the highest quality rice to households, restaurants, and businesses. Coming from a background where rice is not just food but a way of life, we understood the importance of delivering fresh, premium grains that nourish both body and soul.


Driven by passion and a commitment to excellence, we spent years researching and partnering with trusted farmers who share our values of sustainability, ethical farming, and top-tier quality. Every grain we sell is carefully sourced, processed, and handled with the utmost care to ensure that it meets the highest standards of taste, texture, and nutrition.


More Than Just a Business—A Commitment to Quality


Rice is more than just a staple in many households—it’s a symbol of togetherness, culture, and tradition. That’s why we go above and beyond to bring you rice that is not only fresh but also responsibly sourced. We work closely with farmers, supporting ethical agricultural practices that protect both the environment and the livelihoods of those who cultivate the land.


From seed to plate, we oversee every step of the process to guarantee that our rice remains pure, unadulterated, and of the finest quality. Our customers deserve nothing but the best, and we take that responsibility seriously.


Nourishing Families, Supporting Communities


Beyond providing high-quality rice, we are deeply committed to making a difference in the communities we serve. We believe that every grain should contribute to a greater purpose. That’s why we actively support local farmers, invest in sustainable practices, and ensure that our business has a positive impact on both people and the planet.


With every purchase, our customers become part of something bigger—a movement toward better food, fairer trade, and a healthier future. Whether you’re preparing a simple meal at home, running a restaurant, or stocking up for business, we want to be your trusted partner in delivering the best rice for every occasion.


Our Promise to You


As we continue to grow, our mission remains the same: to provide high-quality, nutritious rice that brings comfort and joy to your table. We promise to uphold our values of trust, integrity, and excellence in everything we do.


Thank you for being part of our journey. Together, we are not just sharing meals—we are sharing a legacy of quality, care, and commitment, one grain at a time.
